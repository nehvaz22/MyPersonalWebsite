Welcome to Neha Vazarkar's Website!

Climate Change: A Cause I Care About

Climate change is a severe phenomenon that has the potential to affect people around the world, especially those living in underprivileged and poorer regions. As someone lucky enough to live in New Jersey, I am passionate about spreading climate change awareness and education throughout the developing world to ensure that societies will have adequate resources to combat climate challenges.


What I Have Done to Help:

One of the ways I have helped out is by joining the organization Care About Climate. I joined as a financial and grant writing intern in November 2020, writing three successful grants that funded over $20,000 for climate change and sustainability education for women and minorities in the developing world. In March, I became the program director of the Climate Sign, which is a program which aims to create a global sign for climate change (similar in idea to the peace sign.) The climate sign is formed as a hand curved into a C, and was featured on the Eiffel Tower a few years ago! My work so far has been creating the website through Squarespace and coming up with a marketing plan.
